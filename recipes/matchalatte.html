Matcha Latter

Description

A matcha latte is a tea latte made with green tea powder and a milk of your choice

Brilliant green color and lightly bitter flavor. Try iced or hot!

Ingredients

  • Matcha
  • Milk
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Whisk matcha in a bowl to make it soft
  2. Fill glass with ice and add milk to make it 3/4 way full
  3. Add matcha to glass and mix. Enjoy!
